# How To Play License Plate Bingo

\<Play Now\> \<Summary\>  
License Plate Bingo is a fun road trip game where players watch for license plates from different states and mark them on a bingo card. It turns long drives into a scavenger hunt and is a great way to keep passengers entertained.  
Growing up, my family went on a lot of road trips. We vacationed at least once a year and very seldom flew. Since we were in the car so long, we needed to find things to do. One game that we found was the license plate game.   
To play the game, we were divided into two teams. The way you play is simple: if you see a license plate that is not the state you're currently driving through, you call it out. The first team to call out each state gets that state. At the end of the trip, the team with the most states won.

## The License Plate Game as Bingo

Stories aside, I would like to offer a new way to play the license plate game: License Plate Bingo.   
Instead of competing for the most states, players will try to complete their bingo card of random states. Whenever you see a license plate from a state that you are not currently driving through, call it out and mark it on your bingo card.   
Once a vehicle is called, it should not be called again by someone else. For the sake of keeping it fun, do not worry too much about tracking which vehicles have already been counted.  
The goal is simple: be the first player to complete a bingo pattern on your card.

## Custom Rules and Variations

There are several ways you can customize the rules of the game.  
For example, when a state is called, you could allow everyone with that state on their card to mark it off. Alternatively, you could play such that only the player who called the state gets to mark it.  
If you choose the second option, consider adding rules that prevent players from blocking others, For  instance, you might prevent players from repeatedly calling the same state or calling states that do not appear on their bingo card.  
You can also introduce some fun rules, such as foreign license plates counting as wild spaces.  
There are so many ways the game can be played. Feel free to experiment and create rules that make the game enjoyable for your group. Consider the suggestions here as simply a starting point.

## Suggested License Plate Bingo Rules

* You cannot call out license plates from the state you’re currently driving through  
* Two people cannot call out the same license plate from a vehicle unless it is passed again later  
* Optional: Only the person that called a plate can mark it on their card  
* Optional: You cannot call out a license plate that you already marked, or is not on your bingo card.  
* Optional: Foreign plates are wild

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can the driver play?

I would not suggest that the driver plays. Their job is to focus on the road and get everyone to the destination safely. The co-pilot can play, but they should also remain vigilant as a second pair of eyes for the driver.  
Theoretically, the driver could play if someone else manages their card for them, do so at your own risk. Please stay safe on the roads\!
